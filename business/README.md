## About Anvils

Anvils is a coordination layer between founders and their professionals (CAs, CSs, Auditors) — handling incorporation, equity management, and compliance tracking in one platform. Phase 1 launches with Private Limited, LLP, and OPC. The platform serves founders, chartered accountants, company secretaries, and investors.

## Revenue Streams

1. **Incorporation** (one-time) — Platform fee Rs 999-7,999 + government fees + stamp duty + DSC
2. **Platform SaaS** (recurring) — Starter Rs 499/mo (all companies), Growth Rs 2,999/mo (equity/fundraising), Scale Rs 9,999/mo (governance)
3. **Services Marketplace** (per-transaction) — 50+ à la carte services fulfilled by partner CAs/CSs/Auditors, 15-20% platform service margin

## Operating Model

- **Phase 1** (Month 1-6): 3 people, Rs 2.5-3L/mo burn, Pvt Ltd + LLP + OPC only
- **Break-even**: Month 10-14 at lean ops
- **Capital required**: Rs 30-50L (founder-fundable)
