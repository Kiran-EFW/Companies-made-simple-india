## About Anvils

Anvils handles the full company lifecycle in India — from incorporation through fundraising to ongoing regulatory compliance — in one integrated platform. It serves founders, chartered accountants, company secretaries, and investors across 7 entity types and all 28 Indian states.

## Revenue Streams

1. **Incorporation** (one-time) — Platform fee + government fees + stamp duty + DSC
2. **Compliance Subscriptions** (recurring) — Monthly/annual plans from Rs 999 to Rs 9,999/month
3. **Services Marketplace** (per-service) — 50+ add-on services across 6 categories
