# Glossary

Definitions of Indian regulatory, financial, equity, tax, and platform-specific terms used across the Anvils platform.

---

## Indian Regulatory & Government Bodies

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|------|-----------|------------|
| **MCA** | Ministry of Corporate Affairs | Central government ministry responsible for company registration, regulation, and governance in India |
| **ROC** | Registrar of Companies | State-level officer under MCA who handles company registration and annual filings |
| **RD** | Regional Director | MCA officer who grants licenses for Section 8 companies |
| **CBDT** | Central Board of Direct Taxes | Government body administering income tax and TDS |
| **CBIC** | Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs | Government body administering GST |
| **RBI** | Reserve Bank of India | Central bank regulating foreign investment (FEMA compliance) |
| **SEBI** | Securities and Exchange Board of India | Regulator for securities markets and public companies |
| **DPIIT** | Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade | Manages Startup India recognition and benefits |
| **ROF** | Registrar of Firms | State-level authority for partnership firm registration |
| **EPFO** | Employees' Provident Fund Organisation | Government body managing provident fund for employees |
| **ESIC** | Employees' State Insurance Corporation | Government body managing employee health insurance |

## Company & Entity Types

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **PLC / Pvt Ltd** | Private Limited Company — most common entity for funded startups (min 2 directors, 2 shareholders) |
| **OPC** | One Person Company — single-director, single-shareholder entity for solo founders |
| **LLP** | Limited Liability Partnership — hybrid entity with limited liability and partnership flexibility |
| **Section 8 Company** | Non-profit company under Section 8 of Companies Act, 2013 — for charitable purposes |
| **Sole Proprietorship** | Unregistered business owned by a single individual — simplest entity type |
| **Partnership Firm** | Business owned by 2+ partners governed by a partnership deed |
| **Public Limited** | Company with shares tradeable to the public (min 7 shareholders, 3 directors) |
| **Producer Company** | Entity for agricultural producers and cooperatives (future support) |
| **Nidhi Company** | Mutual benefit society for members' financial needs (future support) |

## Company Governance

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **CIN** | Corporate Identity Number — unique identifier assigned to every registered company |
| **LLPIN** | LLP Identification Number — unique identifier for LLPs |
| **DIN** | Director Identification Number — unique identifier for company directors |
| **DPIN** | Designated Partner Identification Number — unique identifier for LLP designated partners |
| **DSC** | Digital Signature Certificate — electronic signature required for MCA filings (Class 2/3) |
| **MOA** | Memorandum of Association — fundamental document defining company's objects and powers |
| **AOA** | Articles of Association — document defining company's internal rules and regulations |
| **AGM** | Annual General Meeting — mandatory yearly meeting of shareholders |
| **EGM** | Extraordinary General Meeting — special shareholder meeting for urgent matters |
| **Board Resolution** | Formal decision made by the board of directors at a board meeting |
| **Quorum** | Minimum number of directors/shareholders needed for a valid meeting |
| **Statutory Audit** | Mandatory annual audit of financial statements by a qualified CA |
| **Secretarial Audit** | Audit of compliance with company law — mandatory for public/large companies |
| **COI** | Certificate of Incorporation — document proving company's legal existence |

## Equity & Cap Table

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Cap Table** | Capitalization table — record of all company shareholders and their ownership percentages |
| **Authorized Capital** | Maximum share capital a company is authorized to issue (set in MOA) |
| **Paid-Up Capital** | Actual amount of capital paid by shareholders for shares issued |
| **Face Value** | Nominal value of a share as stated in the MOA (typically Rs 10) |
| **Premium** | Amount paid above face value when shares are issued |
| **Promoter** | Founding shareholder who controls the company — identified in MCA filings |
| **Share Transfer** | Transfer of shares from one shareholder to another (Form SH-4) |
| **Share Allotment** | Issuance of new shares to investors/employees (Form PAS-3) |
| **Buyback** | Company repurchasing its own shares from shareholders |
| **Dilution** | Reduction in existing shareholders' ownership percentage due to new share issuance |

## ESOP (Employee Stock Options)

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **ESOP** | Employee Stock Option Plan — scheme allowing employees to buy company shares at a predetermined price |
| **Option Pool** | Total number of shares reserved for ESOP grants |
| **Grant** | Specific allocation of options to an individual employee |
| **Vesting** | Process by which employees earn the right to exercise options over time |
| **Cliff** | Minimum period before any options vest (typically 12 months) |
| **Exercise** | Act of buying shares by paying the exercise price |
| **Exercise Price** | Price at which an employee can buy shares under their ESOP grant |
| **Lapse** | Forfeiture of unvested options (typically when an employee leaves) |
| **Section 80-IAC** | Tax provision allowing DPIIT-recognized startups to defer ESOP tax for 5 years |

## Fundraising & Investment

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Pre-Money Valuation** | Company valuation before new investment money comes in |
| **Post-Money Valuation** | Company valuation after new investment (pre-money + investment amount) |
| **Price Per Share** | Post-money valuation divided by total shares outstanding |
| **SAFE** | Simple Agreement for Future Equity — convertible instrument with no interest or maturity date |
| **CCD** | Compulsorily Convertible Debentures — debt that must convert to equity within a set period |
| **CCPS** | Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares — preference shares that must convert to equity |
| **Convertible Note** | Debt instrument that converts to equity on a future event (usually next funding round) |
| **Valuation Cap** | Maximum valuation at which a SAFE/convertible note converts to equity |
| **Discount Rate** | Percentage discount on the next round's share price for SAFE/note holders |
| **Term Sheet** | Non-binding document outlining key terms of an investment |
| **SHA** | Shareholder Agreement — binding agreement between shareholders on governance and rights |
| **SSA** | Share Subscription Agreement — agreement for subscribing to new shares |
| **Data Room** | Secure repository of company documents shared with potential investors during due diligence |
| **Closing Room** | Final stage of a funding round where documents are signed and funds are transferred |
| **FMV** | Fair Market Value — valuation of shares as per Rule 11UA for tax purposes |
| **Rule 11UA** | Income Tax rule specifying methods for determining FMV of unquoted shares |
| **DCF** | Discounted Cash Flow — valuation method based on projected future cash flows |

## Tax & Compliance

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **GST** | Goods and Services Tax — indirect tax on supply of goods and services |
| **GSTR-1** | Monthly return of outward supplies (sales) |
| **GSTR-3B** | Monthly summary return with tax payment |
| **GSTR-9** | Annual GST return |
| **TDS** | Tax Deducted at Source — tax deducted by payer before making payments |
| **TAN** | Tax Deduction Account Number — required for deducting TDS |
| **PAN** | Permanent Account Number — unique 10-character tax identifier |
| **ITR** | Income Tax Return — annual filing of income and tax paid |
| **Advance Tax** | Quarterly installment payments of estimated annual tax liability |
| **Section 194A** | TDS on interest (other than on securities) — 10% |
| **Section 194C** | TDS on contractor payments — 1% (individual) / 2% (others) |
| **Section 194J** | TDS on professional/technical fees — 10% |
| **FEMA** | Foreign Exchange Management Act — regulates foreign investment in India |
| **HSN** | Harmonized System of Nomenclature — code for classifying goods under GST |
| **SAC** | Services Accounting Code — code for classifying services under GST |
| **DLT** | Distributed Ledger Technology — India's SMS regulatory registration system |

## Professional Roles

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **CA** | Chartered Accountant — professional who handles tax, audit, and financial compliance |
| **CS** | Company Secretary — professional responsible for corporate governance and MCA compliance |
| **Auditor** | CA appointed to conduct statutory audit of financial statements |
| **Designated Partner** | LLP partner with legal responsibility for compliance (similar to director in a company) |
