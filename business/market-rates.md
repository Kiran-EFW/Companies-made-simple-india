# Market Rates — Professional Services & Incorporation Costs in India

Comparative pricing analysis across major online legal/CA platforms and the Anvils marketplace. All prices in INR. Rates surveyed March 2026 from publicly listed platform pricing pages.

> **How to read this document**: "Market Range" reflects the professional/platform fee charged by online service providers (not government fees, which are listed separately). "Anvils Fee" is the platform fee charged on the Anvils marketplace. Government fees are pass-through at actual cost.
