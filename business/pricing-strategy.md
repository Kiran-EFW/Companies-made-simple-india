## Part 5: The Indian Market Reality

### Why This Market Is Different

1. **Price anchoring is low**: Founders expect to pay Rs 5,000-10,000 for incorporation, not Rs 20,000. Anything above Rs 15,000 (total) feels expensive.

2. **Compliance is seen as a cost, not a value**: Indian SMEs view compliance filings the way people view paying electricity bills — necessary but not valued. They will always shop for the cheapest option unless you give them a reason not to.

3. **Trust matters more than price**: Indian business owners will pay Rs 10,000 more to a CA they trust than save Rs 10,000 on an online platform they don't know. Trust is earned through referrals, not ads.

4. **CAs control the market**: 80% of Indian companies are managed by a CA. The CA is the trusted advisor. If the CA doesn't recommend your platform, the founder won't use it.

5. **Switching cost is high (once locked in)**: Once a company's compliance history, documents, and filings are on a platform, switching is painful. This creates natural retention — if you can get them in.

6. **Payment behavior**: Indian SMEs prefer annual lump-sum over monthly subscriptions. Monthly payments feel like a drain. Annual feels like "one and done."

7. **Regulatory complexity creates demand**: India has 50+ compliance deadlines per year for a simple Pvt Ltd. This complexity is your moat — no founder can manage this alone.

## Part 6: Recommended Pricing Strategy

### Strategy: "Wedge & Lock" — Low Entry, High Retention

The core insight: **Incorporation is the wedge, compliance is the lock.** Price the wedge aggressively low. Make the lock indispensable.

### Move 1: Make Incorporation Near-Free

**Current**: Rs 4,999 (Launch) for Private Limited
**Recommended**: Rs 1,999 for Pvt Ltd incorporation (limited period), Rs 0 if they commit to an annual compliance plan

**Rationale**:
- Incorporation costs us ~Rs 1,500 in professional time
- At Rs 1,999 we make Rs 499 margin — barely profitable, but the customer is now IN our system
- At Rs 0 (with annual compliance commitment), we forgo Rs 4,999 one-time but gain Rs 49,999/year in recurring revenue
- Rs 0 incorporation is a nuclear competitive advantage — no one else offers this
- The customer's company data, documents, cap table, compliance history — all live on Anvils. Switching cost is enormous.

**Implementation**:
- Keep the 3-tier structure (Launch/Grow/Scale) for customers who want incorporation only
- Add a "Free Incorporation" option that requires 12-month compliance plan commitment (paid upfront)
- Display as: "Rs 0 incorporation when you choose an annual compliance plan"

### Move 2: Restructure Compliance Plans for Indian Price Points

**Current problem**: Scale plan at Rs 4,999/month (Rs 49,999/year) is the right plan for Pvt Ltd but feels expensive. The Starter plan at Rs 999/month loses money.

**Recommended restructure**:

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Target | Change |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | Rs 1,499 | Rs 14,999 | Sole Prop, Partnership | Price up from Rs 999 to cover costs |
| **Essential** (new) | Rs 2,499 | Rs 24,999 | Pvt Ltd (basic compliance only) | New tier — ROC + ITR + DIR-3 KYC only |
| **Growth** | Rs 3,999 | Rs 39,999 | LLP, OPC, Pvt Ltd (with GST) | Slight increase, add GST filing |
| **Scale** | Rs 5,999 | Rs 59,999 | Pvt Ltd (full service) | Price up — includes bookkeeping, audit coordination |
| **Enterprise** | Rs 9,999 | Rs 99,999 | Public Ltd, Section 8 | No change |

**Key change**: Add an "Essential" plan at Rs 2,499/month (Rs 24,999/year) that covers ONLY mandatory filings (ROC, ITR, KYC). This matches the competitor price point (Vakilsearch Rs 24,999/yr) and gives a lower-priced entry for Private Limited customers who handle their own GST/bookkeeping.

The Scale plan (Rs 5,999/month) becomes the premium option for customers who want everything managed.

### Move 3: Push Annual Hard, Kill Monthly Churn

**Problem**: Monthly plans have 30-40% annual churn in Indian SaaS. Annual plans have 15-20% churn.

**Tactics**:
- **Discount annual plans aggressively**: Current annual = 10 months (save 2 months). Make it annual = 9 months (save 3 months). Indian customers love the feeling of "getting 3 months free."
- **Default to annual on the pricing page**: Show annual pricing first, monthly as a toggle
- **Annual-only for "Free Incorporation" offer**: Free incorp only with annual compliance plan
- **Auto-renewal reminders**: 60 days before renewal, send compliance health report showing "17 tasks completed, Rs X in penalties avoided"

### Move 4: CA Channel as the Primary Distribution

**Why CAs are the strategy, not just a channel**:

| Metric | Direct (Google Ads) | CA Channel |
|---|---|---|
| CAC per customer | Rs 4,000-10,000 | Rs 200-500 |
| Trust at signup | Low (unknown platform) | High (CA recommended) |
| Churn (Year 1) | 30-40% | 10-15% |
| Avg plan selected | Starter/Growth | Scale/Enterprise |
| Upsell conversion | 15-20% | 40-60% |

**CA-specific pricing**:
- **CA Dashboard**: Free — no cost to the CA to use the platform
- **CA Commission**: 15-20% of the first year's compliance subscription
- **CA Bulk Discount**: 10+ clients = 10% discount on all plans for their clients
- **CA Co-Branding**: Allow CAs to white-label client reports with their firm name

**Goal**: 50 active CAs in Year 1, each managing 10+ clients on Anvils = 500+ compliance subscribers with 10-15% churn.

### Move 5: Unbundle for A La Carte Customers, Bundle for Subscribers

**For subscription customers**: Everything included in the plan is already bundled. No surprises. The customer pays one number and knows their entire compliance is handled.

**For non-subscribers**: Price a la carte services 30-40% higher than what they'd cost inside a subscription. This creates a natural incentive to upgrade:

| Service | Subscriber Price | Non-Subscriber Price | Savings |
|---|---|---|---|
| Annual ROC Filing | Included in plan | Rs 9,999 | 100% saved |
| ITR Filing | Included in plan | Rs 11,999 | 100% saved |
| DIR-3 KYC (2 dirs) | Included in plan | Rs 1,499/dir | Rs 2,998 saved |
| GST Monthly Filing | Included (Growth+) | Rs 2,499/month | Rs 29,988/yr saved |

**Display this on the services page**: "You're paying Rs 42,000/year for individual services. The Scale plan covers all of this for Rs 49,999/year + bookkeeping + audit coordination."

### Move 6: Penalty Protection as a Moat

Indian compliance has real, painful penalties:
- ROC late filing: Rs 100/day per form per officer (no cap)
- GST late filing: Rs 50/day (max Rs 10,000 per return)
- ITR late filing: Rs 5,000-10,000 + interest
- DIR-3 KYC missed: Rs 5,000 + DIN deactivation

**Strategy**: Offer "Penalty Protection" as a premium feature in Scale and Enterprise plans:
- If Anvils misses a filing deadline, we cover the penalty up to Rs 25,000/year as service credits
- This creates real, quantifiable value: "Your compliance plan costs Rs 49,999/year. Without it, missed deadlines could cost you Rs 50,000+ in penalties."
- This is already in the Peace of Mind plan — extend it to Scale plan as well

## Part 8: Pricing Page Recommendations

### How to Display Pricing

**Rule 1: Lead with annual pricing**
Show "Rs 24,999/year" not "Rs 2,499/month". Indian customers respond better to a single annual number than a monthly drain.

**Rule 2: Show total cost of non-compliance**
Next to each plan, show: "Companies without a compliance plan paid an average of Rs 47,000 in penalties last year."

**Rule 3: Free incorporation as the headline**
"Incorporate your Pvt Ltd for Rs 0. Focus on your business — we handle the paperwork."
(Subtext: "Free with annual compliance plan. Standalone incorporation from Rs 4,999.")

**Rule 4: Competitor comparison without naming competitors**
"Other platforms charge Rs 7,000-16,000 for incorporation alone. We include it free with your compliance plan."

**Rule 5: Show what's included, not what's excluded**
Each plan should have a detailed feature list. Don't use strikethroughs or "not included" markers — they create negative impressions.

```
[Hero: "Incorporate Free. Stay Compliant Forever."]

[Toggle: Annual (Save 25%) | Monthly]

[4 Plan Cards]:
  Essential — Rs 24,999/yr — "Mandatory filings only"
```

## Part 10: Summary — The Playbook

1. **Incorporation = Customer Acquisition Cost.** Don't try to make money on incorporation. Make it free or near-free to get customers into the ecosystem.

2. **Compliance subscription = The Business.** Every Pvt Ltd company MUST do annual filings. Make it easy to subscribe, painful to leave. Target Rs 3,500/month blended ARPU.

3. **Annual billing = Default.** Monthly creates churn. Annual creates commitment. Discount aggressively (save 3 months) to push annual.

4. **CAs = Distribution.** Stop competing with CAs — partner with them. A CA with 50 clients is worth Rs 21L/year in revenue. Invest in the CA Portal and referral program.

5. **Penalty protection = Differentiation.** No competitor offers this. It creates quantifiable, real-world value that justifies premium pricing.

6. **Essential plan = Market match.** The Rs 24,999/year essential plan matches competitor pricing and gives price-sensitive Pvt Ltd founders an entry point.

7. **Data lock-in = Moat.** Once a company's incorporation docs, cap table, compliance history, and shareholder register are on Anvils, the switching cost is months of manual work. This is the real moat — not features, not price.

8. **Volume target: 500 subscribers by Month 18.** This is the milestone that makes the business self-sustaining. Everything before this is investment in reaching that number.
