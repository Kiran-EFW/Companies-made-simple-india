# Company Overview

## What Anvils Does

Anvils is the equity, governance, and compliance platform for Indian companies. It serves the full company lifecycle — from incorporation to fundraising to ongoing compliance — combining the product polish of Stripe Atlas, the lifecycle approach of SeedLegals, and deep India-specific regulatory expertise powered by AI with human expert verification.

## The Problem

Starting and running a company in India is fragmented and opaque:

- **Incorporation** requires navigating MCA portals, stamp duty variations across 28 states, DSC procurement, DIN applications, and entity-specific forms (SPICe+, FiLLiP, INC-12). Most founders rely on CAs who charge Rs 15,000-50,000 with little transparency.
- **Ongoing compliance** is a maze of 50+ recurring regulatory obligations — ROC filings, GST returns, TDS quarterly submissions, board meetings, AGMs, statutory audits — each with different deadlines and penalties for non-compliance.
- **Equity management** is handled in spreadsheets. Cap tables, ESOP plans, and fundraising instruments (SAFE, CCD, CCPS) are tracked manually with no single source of truth.
- **Fundraising** involves scattered documents, no standardized data room, and manual investor coordination.
- **Professional advisors** (CAs and CSs) manage multiple clients using WhatsApp and email with no unified dashboard.

## The Solution

Anvils is a single platform where:

- A **founder** can incorporate any entity type, set up their cap table, manage ESOPs, raise funds, stay compliant, sign documents, and order services — all from one dashboard.
- A **CA/CS** can manage compliance calendars, track filings, and handle tax returns across all their assigned companies from a dedicated portal.
- An **investor** can view their portfolio holdings, access data rooms, and discover deals — with or without creating an account.
- An **operations team** can manage the incorporation pipeline, track SLAs, handle fulfillment, and monitor revenue from an admin portal.

## Competitive Positioning

### Traditional CAs/CS Firms
- **Their model**: Manual, relationship-based, opaque pricing, WhatsApp-driven
- **Our advantage**: Transparent pricing, self-serve dashboard, automated compliance calendar, AI-powered document generation

### Online Incorporation Platforms (Vakilsearch, LegalRaasta, IndiaFilings)
- **Their model**: Incorporation as a service, limited post-incorporation support
- **Our advantage**: Full lifecycle (incorporation + equity + compliance + fundraising), integrated cap table and ESOP, investor portal, AI copilot

### Equity Management Tools (Carta, Qapita)
- **Their model**: Cap table and equity management only, no incorporation or compliance
- **Our advantage**: Incorporation-to-equity pipeline, Indian regulatory compliance built-in, services marketplace

### DIY (MCA Portal Direct)
- **Their model**: Free but complex, error-prone, no guidance
- **Our advantage**: AI-guided entity selection, automated form filling, expert verification, compliance autopilot

## Design Principles

1. **Company-scoped everything** — Every dashboard action happens in the context of a selected company
2. **Role-based, not app-based** — One dashboard shell, sidebar adapts to role (founder, CA, investor)
3. **No dead ends** — Empty states always offer a next action
4. **Payment where the intent is** — Service purchases happen in context, not on a disconnected page
5. **Progressive disclosure** — Show what's relevant to the company's current state
6. **Clean, professional aesthetic** — B2B SaaS design for credibility
