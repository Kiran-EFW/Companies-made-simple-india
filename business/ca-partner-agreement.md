# Professional Services Partner Agreement

**Between:**
- **Anvils India Private Limited** ("Anvils" / "the Company"), a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at [Address]
- **[Partner Name]** ("Partner" / "the Professional"), a practicing [Chartered Accountant / Company Secretary / Cost Accountant] holding membership number [ICAI/ICSI/ICMAI No.], having their office at [Address]

**Effective Date:** [Date]

---

## Recitals

A. Anvils operates a technology platform ("Anvils Platform") that provides incorporation, compliance management, and related services to businesses in India.

B. The Partner is a qualified professional holding valid membership with [ICAI / ICSI / ICMAI] and is duly authorized to practice [chartered accountancy / company secretaryship / cost accountancy] in India.

C. Anvils wishes to engage the Partner as an independent professional to fulfill certain professional services for clients of Anvils, and the Partner wishes to provide such services, on the terms set out in this Agreement.

D. This Agreement does not constitute a fee-sharing, commission, brokerage, or referral arrangement between the parties. Anvils engages the Partner to fulfill specific professional services and pays the Partner a fulfillment fee for each service delivered. The Partner does not pay Anvils any fees, commissions, or share of professional fees.

---

## 1. Definitions

**"Client"** means a company, LLP, partnership, or other business entity that has contracted with Anvils for services through the Anvils Platform.

**"Fulfillment Fee"** means the amount payable by Anvils to the Partner for each service successfully delivered and accepted, as set out in Schedule A.

**"Service"** means a specific professional service (as listed in Schedule A) that Anvils assigns to the Partner for delivery to a Client.

**"Service Assignment"** means a specific request from Anvils to the Partner to deliver a Service to a specified Client, including the scope of work, timeline, and fulfillment fee.

**"Platform Service Margin"** means the difference between the price charged by Anvils to its Client and the Fulfillment Fee paid to the Partner. This margin is Anvils' revenue for providing the platform, client acquisition, quality assurance, and administrative coordination.

---

## 2. Nature of Relationship

2.1 **Independent Professional.** The Partner is an independent professional and not an employee, agent, or sub-contractor of Anvils. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment relationship, partnership, joint venture, or agency between the parties.

2.2 **No Exclusivity.** The Partner is free to maintain and pursue their own independent practice, serve their own clients, and engage with other platforms or service providers. Anvils does not require exclusivity.

2.3 **No Employment Benefits.** The Partner is not entitled to any employment benefits from Anvils, including but not limited to provident fund, ESI, gratuity, leave, or medical insurance.

2.4 **Professional Autonomy.** The Partner retains full professional autonomy in the manner and method of delivering services. Anvils specifies the scope of work and timeline but does not direct or control the professional judgment of the Partner.

---

## 3. Service Delivery

3.1 **Service Assignment Process.**
   - (a) Anvils assigns a Service to the Partner through the Anvils Platform.
   - (b) The Partner may accept or decline a Service Assignment within 24 hours of receipt.
   - (c) Declining a Service Assignment does not affect the Partner's standing, provided the decline rate does not exceed 30% in any calendar quarter.
   - (d) Once accepted, the Partner commits to delivering the Service within the agreed timeline.

3.2 **Service Standards.** The Partner shall:
   - (a) Deliver all services in accordance with applicable professional standards (ICAI Standards on Auditing, ICAI Accounting Standards, GST law, Income Tax law, Companies Act, as applicable)
   - (b) Maintain current professional qualifications and valid membership with the relevant professional body
   - (c) Ensure accuracy and completeness of all filings, returns, and documents prepared
   - (d) Respond to Client queries (routed through Anvils) within 24 hours on business days
   - (e) Inform Anvils immediately of any delay, error, or regulatory issue that may affect service delivery

3.3 **Quality Review.** Anvils reserves the right to review the quality of deliverables before confirming delivery to the Client. If deliverables do not meet the specified standards, Anvils may request corrections before accepting the Service as delivered.

3.4 **Timeline.** Each Service Assignment includes a delivery timeline. The Partner shall complete the Service within this timeline. If the Partner anticipates a delay, they must notify Anvils at least 48 hours before the deadline with the reason and revised timeline.

---

## 4. Fulfillment Fee and Payment

4.1 **Fulfillment Fee.** For each Service successfully delivered and accepted by Anvils, the Partner shall receive the Fulfillment Fee as specified in Schedule A.

4.2 **Invoicing.** The Partner shall issue a GST-compliant invoice to Anvils for each completed Service (or monthly consolidated invoice, at the Partner's option). The invoice must include:
   - Partner's GSTIN (if registered)
   - SAC code for professional services
   - Service description matching the Service Assignment
   - Fulfillment Fee amount
   - GST amount (18% on professional services)

4.3 **Payment Terms.** Anvils shall pay the Fulfillment Fee within 15 days of receiving a valid invoice, after deducting applicable TDS under Section 194J of the Income Tax Act, 1961 (currently 10%).

4.4 **TDS.** Anvils shall deduct TDS on all payments to the Partner as required by law and shall:
   - File quarterly TDS returns (Form 26Q) including the Partner's payments
   - Issue Form 16A to the Partner within the prescribed timelines
   - Deposit TDS with the government within the prescribed timelines

4.5 **No Other Payments.** The Fulfillment Fee is the sole payment from Anvils to the Partner. No additional fees, bonuses, incentives, or reimbursements are payable unless separately agreed in writing.

4.6 **Fee Revision.** Anvils may revise the Fulfillment Fee schedule (Schedule A) with 30 days' written notice. The revised fees apply to new Service Assignments only, not to assignments already accepted.

---

## 5. Client Relationship

5.1 **Anvils as Service Provider.** The Client's contractual relationship is with Anvils. Anvils is the service provider. The Partner delivers the service on behalf of Anvils.

5.2 **No Direct Client Solicitation.** The Partner shall not directly solicit, contact, or market to any Client introduced through the Anvils Platform for services that compete with Anvils' marketplace offerings, for a period of 12 months from the last Service Assignment involving that Client.

5.3 **Existing Clients.** If the Partner already has an independent relationship with a company that is also an Anvils Client, this Agreement does not restrict that existing relationship. The non-solicitation clause in 5.2 applies only to new Clients introduced through the Anvils Platform.

5.4 **Client Communication.** All communication with Clients regarding Anvils-assigned services shall be routed through the Anvils Platform. The Partner may not provide their personal contact details to Clients for Anvils-assigned work.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

6.1 **Confidential Information.** The Partner shall treat all Client data, financial information, documents, and business information accessed through the Anvils Platform as confidential.

6.2 **Data Protection.** The Partner shall:
   - (a) Use Client data only for the purpose of delivering the assigned Service
   - (b) Not retain copies of Client data beyond what is required by law or professional standards
   - (c) Not share Client data with any third party without Anvils' written consent
   - (d) Implement reasonable security measures to protect Client data in their possession

6.3 **Professional Confidentiality.** This clause is in addition to, and does not replace, the Partner's obligations of confidentiality under their professional code of conduct.

---

## 7. Professional Independence

7.1 **Conflict of Interest.** The Partner shall disclose to Anvils any conflict of interest that may arise in connection with a Service Assignment, including:
   - (a) Financial interest in the Client company
   - (b) Personal relationship with Client directors or shareholders
   - (c) Existing engagement with the Client that may create a conflict

7.2 **Audit Independence.** If the Partner is engaged for statutory audit-related services, the Partner shall not accept any Service Assignment from Anvils for the same Client that could impair audit independence under Section 141 of the Companies Act, 2013 or the ICAI Code of Ethics.

7.3 **Bookkeeping-Audit Separation.** The Partner acknowledges that Anvils will not assign both bookkeeping/tax preparation services and statutory audit services for the same Client to the same Partner firm.

---

## 8. Liability and Indemnification

8.1 **Partner Liability.** The Partner is responsible for the professional accuracy and quality of services delivered. If a service delivered by the Partner contains errors that result in penalties, fines, or losses for the Client or Anvils, the Partner shall:
   - (a) Correct the error at no additional cost
   - (b) Indemnify Anvils for any penalties, fines, or claims arising directly from the Partner's error or negligence

8.2 **Anvils Liability.** Anvils is responsible to the Client for service delivery. If Anvils incurs liability due to the Partner's error, Anvils may recover such amounts from the Partner.

8.3 **Limitation.** The Partner's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total Fulfillment Fees paid to the Partner in the 12 months preceding the claim.

8.4 **Professional Insurance.** The Partner is encouraged (but not required) to maintain professional indemnity insurance.

---

## 9. Term and Termination

9.1 **Term.** This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either party.

9.2 **Termination Without Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice.

9.3 **Termination With Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately if:
   - (a) The other party commits a material breach and fails to cure it within 15 days of notice
   - (b) The other party becomes insolvent or is subject to winding-up proceedings
   - (c) The Partner's professional membership is suspended or revoked
   - (d) The Partner is found guilty of professional misconduct by the relevant professional body

9.4 **Effect of Termination.**
   - (a) All pending Service Assignments accepted before termination shall be completed, and the corresponding Fulfillment Fees shall be paid
   - (b) The confidentiality and non-solicitation obligations survive termination for 12 months
   - (c) The Partner shall return or delete all Client data in their possession within 30 days of termination

---

## 10. Dispute Resolution

10.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of India.

10.2 **Dispute Resolution.** Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall first be attempted to be resolved through negotiation between the parties. If unresolved within 30 days, the dispute shall be referred to arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The arbitration shall be conducted by a sole arbitrator in [City], India, in English.

10.3 **Jurisdiction.** Subject to the arbitration clause, the courts of [City], India shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

---

## 11. General

11.1 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with Schedule A, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter.

11.2 **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only in writing, signed by both parties (email acceptance is sufficient).

11.3 **Severability.** If any provision is found invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force.

11.4 **Notices.** Notices under this Agreement may be sent by email to the addresses registered on the Anvils Platform.

11.5 **Assignment.** The Partner may not assign this Agreement without Anvils' written consent.
