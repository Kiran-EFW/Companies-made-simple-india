The Problem
India's company infrastructure is broken
Every founder, CA, and investor deals with the same painful gaps.
🏛

Opaque Incorporation

Starting a company means navigating MCA portals, stamp duty across 28 states, DSC procurement — most founders rely on CAs charging ₹8,000–25,000 with zero transparency on what is a government fee vs. a service fee.

⏰

50+ Compliance Deadlines/Year

ROC filings, GST returns, TDS, DIR-3 KYC, board meetings, AGM — each with ₹100/day penalties. 2 consecutive years of non-filing = company struck off, directors disqualified for 5 years.

📊

Spreadsheet Equity

Cap tables, ESOPs, fundraising tracked in spreadsheets with no single source of truth. Errors compound at every round. Options: Carta at $3K–10K/yr (US-focused), or hire a CS firm at ₹50K–1.5L/yr.

📱

WhatsApp-Driven Practice

CAs manage multiple companies via WhatsApp and email. No dashboard, no automation, no scalability. Founders have zero visibility into filing status.

One platform. Every stakeholder.
Anvils unifies the full company lifecycle — from Day 0 incorporation to Series A governance — for every user type.

We compete with Carta (equity management), not with CAs (compliance filing). CAs are our marketplace providers, not our competitors. Compliance tracking is free — it creates engagement. Equity tools are the paid product.

Why This Is ₹60L Cheaper Than the Full Ops Model

The original staffing plan called for 11.5 people at launch (₹7.75L/mo) including in-house CAs and CSs. Under the marketplace model, we never hire CAs or CSs. They are free marketplace providers. This saves ₹60L in Year 1 salary costs alone and moves break-even from Month 18–20 to Month 12–14.

3 Hiring Rules

1. Never hire a CA/CS — marketplace partners handle fulfillment at zero fixed cost. 2. Hire ops before engineers — one ops person handles 200+ customers. 3. Revenue-gate every hire — each new person must be justified by ₹1.5L+ monthly revenue they unlock or retain.
