# Vision: Companies Made Simple India

## The Problem

Starting a company in India is a fragmented, opaque, 15-45 day process spanning MCA, NSDL, EPFO, State Revenue, DPIIT, DSC vendors, and banks. Founders face:

- **Choice paralysis**: Pvt Ltd vs OPC vs LLP vs Section 8 — which one?
- **Hidden costs**: Advertised ₹999 turns into ₹15,000+ at checkout
- **Black-box status**: "Your application is being processed" with no visibility
- **Compliance cliff**: Incorporation is Day 1 — then 50+ annual compliance obligations hit

Existing platforms (IndiaFilings, Vakilsearch, ClearTax) are **service companies with dashboards**, not product companies. They wrap CA/CS firms in a web UI.

## The Solution

A unified, AI-native platform that takes entrepreneurs from **0 → 1**:

```
IDEA → ENTITY SELECTION → INCORPORATION → POST-SETUP → COMPLIANCE → GROWTH
         AI Wizard          AI + Team        Autopilot     Autopilot    Ecosystem
```

## Core Principles

1. **AI-First, Human-Verified**: AI handles 80% (document processing, form filling, compliance drafting). Backend CS/CA team verifies the remaining 20%.

2. **Radical Transparency**: Real-time pipeline tracking. Zero markup on government fees. Full itemized cost breakdown before payment.

3. **Lifecycle Platform**: Not just incorporation — we stay with the entrepreneur through compliance, banking, funding, and scaling.

4. **Every Company Type**: Pvt Ltd, OPC, LLP, Section 8, Producer Company, Sole Proprietorship — all under one roof.

## Architecture Model

**The "Sandwich" Model**: AI Drafts → Human Verifies → System Files

AI agents handle document OCR, form auto-fill, name validation, compliance drafting, and the chatbot. The backend operations team (CS professionals, CAs) reviews every output before submission, handles exceptions, and provides expert guidance for edge cases.
